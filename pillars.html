The Pillars

The four pillars, which serve as the foundation of both the science and the techniques, connect all the techniques into a unified whole. All the techniques are built upon these four pillars.

Everything is a game

We need to know that growth mindset. With enough effort and strategy you can get better at anything. Everything is trainable. Intelligence can be developed.

The Feynman Technique

One of my favorites. In order to understand a topic really well, you need to be able to teach it and explain it in a simple way. Bringing down a concept to its basic level. lose the big words and jargon. Speak in simple words.

Trunk Based knowledge

Start at the roots. The fundamentals, the principles. The "why" of something. knowledge in a form of a tree.

Efficiency Trumps Grit

You don't have to learn and work constantly. Everyone is always busy. Be smart and efficient with your time. We have limited time in our lives. limited skills we can acquire. we need to be efficient. Most people don't take the time to learn how to be efficient.
